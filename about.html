Our Story

Craft Passed
Hand to Hand.

The Rug Atelier exists at the intersection of living tradition and contemporary living — a studio committed to preserving the art of handmade rugs for the spaces of today.

Who We Are

A Studio with
Deep Roots.

The Rug Atelier was founded on a simple belief: that the ancient art of rug-making deserves to be experienced in contemporary homes. Each rug we create is a collaboration — between our designers and the master craftspeople whose families have been weaving for generations.

We source the finest natural fibres — hand-combed Ghazni wool, hand-spun silk, plant-based dyes distilled from roots, bark, and minerals. Every material is chosen not only for beauty but for longevity, ensuring that each piece becomes richer with time rather than diminishing.

Our collections are not seasonal. They are enduring. Designed to anchor a space for decades, to outlast trends, and to accumulate the quiet presence that only handmade objects carry.

The Process

From Fibre
to Floor.

01

Material Selection

We travel to source regions to hand-select each fibre — building relationships with shepherds, farmers, and spinners who share our values.

02

Natural Dyeing

Yarns are dyed using centuries-old plant-based techniques, producing deep, complex colours that synthetic dyes simply cannot replicate.

03

Hand Weaving

Skilled artisans work on traditional looms, hand-knotting each row with a precision learned over lifetimes. A single rug can take months.

04

Finishing & Inspection

Each piece is hand-washed, sun-dried, and meticulously inspected before it leaves the atelier. Only then does it earn our mark.
